About GRMS

Graduate Research Management System (GRMS) is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline research workflows at FUUAST.

From synopsis submission to thesis evaluation, GRMS provides a centralized digital environment for students, supervisors, and administrators to collaborate efficiently.
